Arancini (Sicilian Rice Balls)

Evolution

Arancini are believed to have been inspired by the Arab introduction of rice and saffron to Sicily. They evolved into a popular street food, stuffed with ragù, cheese, and peas, showcasing Sicilian ingenuity.

Ingredients

  • Arborio rice
  • Saffron
  • Mozzarella
  • Ragù (meat sauce)
  • Peas
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Eggs
  • Oil for frying

Way of Making

  1. Cook rice with saffron; let cool.
  2. Form rice balls and fill with ragù, peas, and mozzarella.
  3. Coat with beaten egg and breadcrumbs.
  4. Deep-fry until golden and crisp.
