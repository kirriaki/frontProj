Bûche de Noël

About

Bûche de Noël, or Yule Log Cake, is a traditional French dessert served during Christmas. It consists of a light sponge cake rolled with rich chocolate filling and decorated to resemble a festive log.

Ingredients

For the sponge cake:

  • 4 eggs
  • 100g sugar
  • 100g flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

For the filling and frosting:

  • 200g dark chocolate
  • 200ml heavy cream
  • 50g powdered sugar

Way of Making

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F). Line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  2. Beat eggs and sugar until pale and fluffy. Fold in flour and vanilla gently.
  3. Spread the batter evenly on the tray. Bake for 8–10 minutes.
  4. Roll the warm cake with a kitchen towel and let it cool.
  5. For the filling: Melt the chocolate and mix with whipped cream and sugar.
  6. Unroll the cake, spread filling, and roll it back. Cover with frosting and decorate to resemble a log.
