Galette des Rois

About

Galette des Rois, or "King Cake," is a traditional French dessert enjoyed during Epiphany. It features layers of buttery puff pastry filled with a rich almond cream, often hiding a small fève (trinket) inside for good luck.

Ingredients

  • 2 sheets puff pastry
  • 100g almond flour
  • 100g sugar
  • 100g unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp almond extract
  • 1 fève (optional)

Way of Making

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C (375°F).
  2. Beat almond flour, sugar, butter, 1 egg, and almond extract into a smooth paste.
  3. Place one pastry sheet on a baking tray, spread the filling, leaving a 2 cm border. Hide the fève.
  4. Cover with the second pastry sheet, seal edges, and score the top.
  5. Brush with beaten egg and bake for 25–30 minutes until golden.
