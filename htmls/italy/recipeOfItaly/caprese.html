Torta Caprese (Chocolate and Almond Cake)

Evolution

This cake originates from the island of Capri, and legend has it that it was created by mistake when a baker forgot to add flour. Over time, it became a popular gluten-free dessert throughout Italy.

Ingredients

Main Ingredients:

  • Dark chocolate
  • Almonds (ground)
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla extract

Way of Making

  1. Melt the chocolate and butter together, then allow it to cool.
  2. Beat the eggs and sugar until fluffy, then fold in the melted chocolate, ground almonds, and vanilla.
  3. Pour the mixture into a greased pan and bake at 170°C (340°F) for 30-35 minutes.
  4. Cool and dust with powdered sugar before serving.
