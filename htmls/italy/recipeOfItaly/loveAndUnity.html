Spaghetti alle Vongole (Spaghetti with Clams)

Evolution

This iconic Neapolitan dish has been popular for centuries, highlighting the fresh flavors of the sea. Its simplicity and balance between garlic, olive oil, and fresh clams make it a classic favorite on Italian coastlines.

Ingredients

Main Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Fresh clams
  • Garlic
  • White wine
  • Parsley
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Olive oil

Way of Making

  1. Soak and clean the clams, discarding any that do not close when tapped.
  2. Saut√© garlic and red pepper flakes in olive oil.
  3. Add clams and white wine, cover, and cook until clams open.
  4. Toss the cooked spaghetti in the sauce and sprinkle with chopped parsley. Serve immediately.
