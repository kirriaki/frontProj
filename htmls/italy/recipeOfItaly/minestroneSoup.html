Minestrone Soup

Evolution

This hearty vegetable soup dates back to ancient Roman times. Originally a simple dish for the working class, it evolved over centuries to include local and seasonal ingredients and became a national favorite.

Ingredients

  • Mixed vegetables (carrots, celery, zucchini, potatoes)
  • Beans
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Pasta or rice
  • Vegetable broth
  • Olive oil

Way of Making

  1. Saut√© garlic and onion in olive oil.
  2. Add chopped vegetables and cook for a few minutes.
  3. Pour in broth and bring to a boil; simmer for 30-40 minutes.
  4. Add pasta or rice and cook until tender. Season with salt, pepper, and fresh herbs.
