Cotechino con Lenticchie (Sausage with Lentils)

Evolution

A traditional dish eaten on New Year's Eve in Italy, symbolizing prosperity due to the round shape of the lentils. Itâ€™s particularly popular in northern regions like Emilia-Romagna.

Ingredients

Main Ingredients:

  • Cotechino sausage
  • Lentils
  • Carrot
  • Onion
  • Bay leaves
  • Olive oil

Way of Making

  1. Simmer the cotechino sausage for about 2-3 hours until tender.
  2. Cook the lentils with onion, carrot, and bay leaves in a separate pot until soft.
  3. Combine the sausage with the lentils, and cook together for a few minutes.
  4. Serve with a drizzle of olive oil.
