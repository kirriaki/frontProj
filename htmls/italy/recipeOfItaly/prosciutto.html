Prosciutto e Melone (Prosciutto and Melon)

Evolution

This dish goes back to the Roman Empire, where the combination of sweet and salty flavors was popular. It became a classic summer appetizer in Italy.

Ingredients

  • Prosciutto crudo (cured ham)
  • Ripe cantaloupe melon

Way of Making

  1. Slice melon into wedges.
  2. Wrap each wedge with a thin slice of prosciutto.
  3. Serve chilled as an appetizer.
