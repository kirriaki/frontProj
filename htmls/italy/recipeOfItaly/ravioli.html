Ravioli

Evolution

Ravioli has roots in medieval Italy, appearing as early as the 14th century. Originally, it was a simple peasant dish made with leftover fillings. Over time, it evolved into a beloved staple filled with a variety of ingredients.

Ingredients

Pasta Dough:

  • Flour
  • Eggs

Filling:

  • Ricotta cheese
  • Spinach
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Nutmeg

Sauce:

  • Tomato sauce or sage butter sauce

Way of Making

  1. Roll out the pasta dough thinly.
  2. Place the filling (ricotta, spinach, Parmesan, nutmeg) in small mounds on the dough.
  3. Cover with another sheet of pasta, press around the fillings, and cut into shapes.
  4. Boil in salted water for a few minutes until cooked.
  5. Serve with your choice of tomato sauce or sage butter sauce.
