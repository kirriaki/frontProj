Pollo alla Cacciatora (Hunter’s Chicken)

Evolution

This traditional Italian dish is said to have originated in rural Tuscany, where hunters would prepare a hearty meal using available ingredients after a long day. It evolved over time to become a comforting, homey dish enjoyed across Italy.

Ingredients

Main Ingredients:

  • Chicken pieces
  • Tomatoes
  • Bell peppers
  • Garlic
  • Rosemary
  • White wine
  • Black olives

Way of Making

  1. Brown chicken pieces in olive oil, then set them aside.
  2. Sauté garlic, bell peppers, and rosemary; deglaze with white wine.
  3. Add tomatoes and black olives, then return chicken to the pan.
  4. Simmer for 30-40 minutes until chicken is tender and flavors have melded.
